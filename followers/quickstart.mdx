With Mellowtel you can invite your followers and fans to support you by adding a plugin or installing a free app.
They can share their bandwidth that is used by companies to access the Web to retrieve public web data. They pay for it and Mellowtel shares part of the revenue with the creator they want to support.

As an estimate for 1000 users that support you, you can make around $50/month (the exact amount depends on a few other variables like geolocation)

In exchange you can give them access to exclusive or premium content through the Mellowtel platform.
